Minesweeper

Description

Classic Minesweeper, play until you have uncovered every space except all the mines.

Controls

Use mouse to control everything. Instructions found in game. Trouble getting the canvas to stay? Try clicking on the canvas and pressing space.

Credits

Made May 2023 - September 2024, by Pineapples. The Original Graphics are copied from the original Minesweeper, released by Microsoft, to whom I have absolutely no association. You can get Microsoft Minesweeper for FREE at their website. HOWEVER, the code containing the pixel sprites does not belong to them, and was reprogrammed by me.
